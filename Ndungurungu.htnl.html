ABOUT THE CONVOCATION

Overview of the seventh convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical
Training centre

The seventh convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre of the year 2024 continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians,students,alumin,benefactors and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university community it is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching research, and professinal development.

This year"s convocation has selected the topic The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and suitsble development in Tanzaniaas the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year"s 18 November African Statistics Day.The theme states mordernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA):The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Africa

African Statistics Day is celebrated each year on 18th of November.The commemoration aims among others,to increase public awareness about the important role which statistics play in all aspects of social and economic development.This year"s convocation aims to understand the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) and the potential role statistics and big data plays in discovering markets and serving customers across Africa
